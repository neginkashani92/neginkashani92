Summer in Quebec City

Québec, the capital of the province, is a prime destination for discovering some of Canada's oldest history and architecture. The city exudes a charming European ambiance, particularly in Old Québec, a historic neighborhood recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1985. Some more of the city’s most famous landmarks include the Château Frontenac and Jacques-Cartier National Park.
